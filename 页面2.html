Pickball in the U.S.

Pickleball is on the rise. In the United States, approximately 2.5 million people play the sport year-round. Pickleball has been one of the regular physical program in high schools. Besides it is getting popular among young and old alike. In New York State alone, more than 500 schools have set it as part of their curriculum. There are municipal, state and national tournaments and national pétanque tournaments across the U.S. each year.
